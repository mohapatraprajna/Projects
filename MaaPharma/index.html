About us

Maa Pharmaceuticals,Cuttack is an established pharmaceutical distributor company whose main activity is to wholesale and export pharmaceutical products.The company purchases its pharmaceutical supplies either directly from the manufacturer or from the manufacturers’officially appointed distributor. We as a distributor and Exporter believe in providing the best customer Service, in terms of Quality,Cost and Timelines. We are rapidly moving towards leadership riding on its success in the emerging and developing pharmaceutical market in Odisha as well as India.
